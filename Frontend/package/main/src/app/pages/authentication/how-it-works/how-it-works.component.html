How It Works

IDentify




Signing up to a business

When joining a business, you can simply scan the provided QR code instead of filling out traditional forms.

This method simplifies the joining process and saves time for both the you and the business.

Uploading an Image

Uploading an image is a simple as selecting the upload button and choosing an image from whatever operating system IDentify is being used on.



Processing an Image

Once the image is uploaded, select the "Submit for Processing" button, IDentify will then analyse the card and retrieve the required information.

You may also remove the uploaded image by selecting "Remove Image".

Confirming Accurate Data is Recorded

You will be presented with a table of information scanned from your ID card, this can be edited by hitting the "Expand" button to ensure the captured information is correct.






How Stored Data is Used

After your information is recorded, the business can then use your information to sign you into their service.
