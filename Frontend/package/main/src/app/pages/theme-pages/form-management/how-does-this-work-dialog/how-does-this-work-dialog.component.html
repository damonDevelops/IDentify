Edit Your Form

To use the IDentify's form management, you'll need to provide a word document (.docx) file. To have IDentify automatically fill in fields for you, you'll need to edit your document to include the neccessary information.

Values:

  • Name: {name}
  • Date of Birth: {dob}
  • Address: {address}
  • License Number: {license_no}
  • Card Number: {card_number}

Upload Your Document

Upload Your Document

Select 'Create New Form'. You'll be able to provide a form name, this is important as it is what will show up when you wish to use this form in future use. Name the form, then select the 'Upload Word Document Button'. Select the form you just edited which contains fields IDentify can recognise, then press create.

Use Your Form for Any Customer

Using Your Form

To use any uploaded form for a new or existing customer, simply head to 'Scan History', select the collection point your customer is saved within, find the customer by using the search bar and click on the 'Select Form' button. Select the form you wish to use in the dropdown menu, and select whether you wish to save the customer filled in form as a PDF or as a Word Document
